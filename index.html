The Best Days

The first 10 days of Dhul Hijjah are a truly blessed time, a time when Allah shows mercy and blesses our righteous deeds with limitless rewards. During this time it’s especially important to fast, pray to Allah (SWT) and to give charity. The charity you give during these beloved days and the mercy you show will go a long way to not only help provide relief for those in need, but to also earn your blessings for the hereafter.

The blessings of Dhul Hijjah are limitless.
